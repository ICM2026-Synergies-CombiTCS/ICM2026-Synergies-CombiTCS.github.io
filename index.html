Synergies in Combinatorics and Theoretical Computer Science

A satellite conference of ICM 2026, bringing together researchers across combinatorics and theoretical computer science.

Dates: August 3–August 7, 2026 Venue: Merkin Center (Caltech) Location: Caltech campus, Pasadena, CA

Organizers

  • David Conlon (Caltech)
  • Irit Dinur (Weizmann and IAS)
  • Jacob Fox (Stanford)
  • Raghu Meka (UCLA)
  • Huy Tuan Pham (Caltech)

Venue

The conference will take place at the Merkin Center on the Caltech campus.
